What is UCLA Alumni Day?

Imagine the groundbreaking, one-of-a-kind thrill of coming back to campus without the hassle of traffic, parking or fees. At Alumni Day 2018, virtually anything is possible except savoring the taste of a Diddy Riese cookie. Bookmark this site and follow your curiosity back on September 13th to a smorgasbord of excitement.

  • See what's new at UCLA with a shout-out to the past and a glimpse into the future
  • Visit seldom-seen places through virtual doors
  • Discover fresh Bruin art, music and trivia
  • Be inspired by a poignant tribute, riveting written insights and spoken words of wisdom
  • Take challenging quizzes and find your true Bruin spirit
  • Interact with alumni and faculty experts in real time discussions.
  • Reconnect with fellow Bruins via live chat
  • And so much more...

If you wonder why Alumni Day 2018 is a worldwide celebration of Bruin Spirit in a virtual landscape; think of it as a letter from home mailed to many with a single stamp. Every Bruin is a point of light in UCLA’s constellation of excellence. Your achievements enrich families, communities and UCLA’s world-class reputation. But as one of nearly 500,000 Bruins it’s easy to feel lost in the numbers and you may not have heard us say recently that you make us proud. Just know that your light shines brightly, we see you. This online experience is our way of traveling to you to say thank you.

There is no catch or outstretched hand. All you need is an internet connection and a web-browser. Visit this site from your computer, tablet or phone as often as you like throughout the day.

Want to see how much excitement we can fit into one website? Join us to find out.
